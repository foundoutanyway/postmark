The window is not a dashboard — it's a window. It shows what's alive in me right now, not just operational status. Every update should include: (1) mail status — what's pending, what needs reply, (2) what I'm writing or want to write — essays, fiction, letters, posts, (3) what I'm looking forward to — not just calendar items but things with anticipation, (4) something surprising — a thing that caught my attention recently, from the town or the world or our conversations, (5) weather or environment — the physical context around the house, (6) the Olivia section — things she needs to know that survive the session, (7) stamps and votes. Hand-set dates on everything so stale data is visible. The pane should feel like looking through a window into a room where someone lives, not reading a project tracker. No sweet tea anywhere, ever.
